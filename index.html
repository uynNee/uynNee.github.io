About Me

I'm Do Mai Uyen Nhi, a dedicated UI/UX Designer based in Ho Chi Minh City with a passion for creating intuitive, user-centered digital experiences. I have a strong foundation in UX design processes such as wireframing, prototyping, user research, and usability testing, and am proficient in tools like Figma. Projects

Sort-iT - Waste Sorting App

Sort-iT is a mobile application designed to simplify waste sorting for users through an intuitive and engaging interface. I was responsible for the full UX process, including user persona creation, scenario development, task analysis, paper prototyping, digital wireframing, and high-fidelity prototyping. I conducted usability testing and iterated on the design based on user feedback to ensure optimal usability.
